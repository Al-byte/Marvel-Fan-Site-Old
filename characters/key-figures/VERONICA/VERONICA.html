Veronica is a mobile service module deployed from a space satellite. It was designed by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner to respond to a Hulk rampage. It is stocked with automatic assembled parts of the Iron Man Armor: Mark XLIV for real time customization of the Hulkbuster armor and versatile combat capability, as well as walls of a self assembled electrified cage whose sum form a containment prison.

With Hulk hypnotized by Scarlet Witch to go on a rampage in the streets of Johannesburg, Tony Stark deployed Veronica to the Hulk's location to help subdue Hulk. Veronica trapped Hulk in its self-assembling electrified cage, while Stark donned the Hulkbuster armor. Hulk broke from his cage and did battle with Stark, with Veronica hovering close by to provide Stark support. Veronica continued to provide real time repair and customization of Stark's Hulkbuster armor by sending flying assembling parts to arm Stark's armor. Eventually, Veronica crash-landed when the Hulk struck one of the flying parts it deployed back to Veronica, thus suffering heavy damage from the collision.
