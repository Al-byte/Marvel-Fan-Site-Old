N'Jadaka, also known as Erik Stevens, was a former United States Navy SEAL of Wakandan origin through his father, the Prince N'Jobu. N'Jadaka's savagery while serving with a U.S. military black-ops unit earned him the nickname Killmonger. When Killmonger was a child, T'Chaka killed his father for trying to murder Zuri for outing him after betraying Wakanda. This drove Killmonger to dedicate his life to forcibly dethroning his cousin, T'Challa, in order to accomplish his father's plan to seize control of the world by using all of Wakanda's Vibranium technology to cease the oppression of people of African descent all across the world.
